OAKSTERDAM UNIVERSITY

Finca has been affiliated with Oaksterdam University for some time now. We have helped with maintaining a high level of education to which we dispense to the community and the world. Oaksterdam has graduated over 35,000 students from over 30 different countries. In addition to dispensing education, we have helped with maintaining the Horticulture department to assure the highest quality of Medical cannabis plants from every single stage of life and all of the wonderful harvested flowers are then dried, cured, and donated to a particular Multiple sclerosis patient.

PROCESS

Cultivation of the Cannabis plant is a process that is followed with a fine and detailed outline. Finca practices Organic, Veganic, and No-Till Organic methods. Whether we are initiating an indoor cultivation or a greenhouse/light deprivation/outdoor cultivation, we follow a standard of policies and procedures that were developed over years of trial and error. Finca follows strict guidelines which correlate with most standard industry guidelines, because our number one priority is YOU, the medical patient/consumer.
